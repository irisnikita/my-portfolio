## Problem

Case studies tend to drift into walls of text.

## Approach

A strict structure:

- **Problem**: context + constraints
- **Approach**: decisions + tradeoffs
- **Result**: outcomes + links

## Result

A consistent, scannable story that’s easy to maintain.
